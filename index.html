About our Team

We are a group of 5 Foundation Year students from City, University of London and we decided to develop a website containing a time Management tool for students in order to improve our studying time to successfully achieve tasks. We have worked together on this website in order to develop a professional-looking website but also to develop our teamwork and time Management skills. Working together has been challenging but extremely rewarding.
